Our Story

Welcome to Bricklab – where LEGO® dreams come to life without the hefty price tag. I’m Ryan, a lifelong LEGO enthusiast and the founder of Bricklab. After months of selling LEGO sets on Vinted (with amazing feedback!), I decided to launch my own website to help fans save even more.

At Bricklab, you’ll find 100% authentic LEGO® sets at prices lower than retail, carefully sourced and shipped with love. Whether you're hunting for a rare find, a birthday gift, or your next build, I'm here to make it easy and affordable.

Thank you for supporting this growing community of builders. Happy building!

How It Works

1. Choose Your Sets

Select the LEGO® sets you want and the quantity you need. You can adjust the quantities easily from the product page.

2. Checkout and Submit Details

Once you're happy with your cart, head to checkout and enter your name, email address, and shipping details. No payment is taken yet!

3. Confirmation and Contact

Your order details will be sent directly to me. I’ll then get in touch via your preferred method — either by email or text — to arrange delivery and postage options.

4. Arrange Postage

We mainly use InPost lockers for smaller orders and Royal Mail Tracked services for larger or more urgent shipments.

5. Invoice and Payment

After confirming the final shipping costs, I’ll send you an invoice to pay securely. Regular updates will be sent until your sets arrive!

Shipping & Delivery

Smaller orders are usually sent via InPost lockers for convenience. Larger or more urgent orders are shipped using Royal Mail Tracked services.

If I have the sets in stock, your order will usually be dispatched within 2 business days. If I need to order the sets from my supplier, it may take around 4–7 days to fulfil your order. However, I always aim to have every order sent out within 3 working days whenever possible! Rest assured, you’ll receive regular updates and full tracking details once your package is on the way.

Shipping costs depend on the size of your order and your chosen delivery method. I’ll always aim to keep it affordable. Here's a guide:

  • InPost (to Locker or Shop):
    - £1.99 – Small parcel (up to 8 x 38 x 64 cm, Max 15kg)
    - £2.59 – Medium parcel (up to 19 x 38 x 64 cm, Max 15kg)
    - £3.99 – Large parcel (up to 41 x 38 x 64 cm, Max 15kg)
  • InPost (to Home Address):
    - £2.89 – Small parcel (up to 8 x 38 x 64 cm, Max 15kg)
    - £3.99 – Medium parcel (up to 19 x 38 x 64 cm, Max 15kg)
    - £5.99 – Large parcel (up to 41 x 38 x 64 cm, Max 15kg)
  • Royal Mail Tracked 24/48:
    Small Parcel (up to 45 x 35 x 16 cm, up to 2kg)
    - £3.45 – Tracked 48
    - £4.29 – Tracked 24

    Medium Parcel (up to 61 x 46 x 46 cm, up to 2kg)
    - £5.15 – Tracked 48
    - £6.05 – Tracked 24

    Heavier Parcels (5–20kg)
    - 5–10kg: £6.80 (Tracked 48) | £7.90 (Tracked 24)
